Erin Lloyd's Biography

My name is Erin Lloyd, and I am currently a sophomore at Syracuse University’s School of Information Studies pursuing a degree in Applied Data Analytics. Within my major, I am curious to do work in many industries, and find out which is best suited to my interests, but right now I am most interested in healthcare analytics.

In my time at Syracuse University, I have been awarded the Leadership Scholarship, which grants me half-off of tuition, as well as I have been on the Dean’s List every semester that I have studied here. Additionally, I earned the Invest in Success Scholarship, which is given to any first-year student that earns a 3.7 or higher during their first semester.
